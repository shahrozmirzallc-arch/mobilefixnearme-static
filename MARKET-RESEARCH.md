## 📞 **CUSTOMER JOURNEY**

### **How People Find & Choose Repair Services:**

**Step 1:** Search on Google
   - "phone repair near me" or "[device] repair [city]"

**Step 2:** Scan results (6 seconds!)
   - Look at reviews (4+ stars minimum)
   - Check photos
   - See if you're close/convenient

**Step 3:** Visit website (30 seconds MAX)
   - Can I afford it? (pricing)
   - Can I trust them? (reviews)
   - How fast? (timeframe)
   - How do I contact? (click-to-call)

**Step 4:** Contact
   - Call or chat
   - Ask about price & timing
   - Book appointment

**Step 5:** Decision
   - Choose based on: price, speed, convenience, trust
