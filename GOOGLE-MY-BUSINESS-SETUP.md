## ⚡ **COMMON ISSUES & SOLUTIONS**

### **Issue: Verification postcard not received**
**Solution:** Request new postcard after 7 days, or try phone/video verification

### **Issue: Address shows publicly**
**Solution:** Make sure you selected SAB (Service Area Business) type. Contact Google support to hide.

### **Issue: Can't add service areas**
**Solution:** Must be SAB type. Go to Info → Address → Edit → "I deliver goods and services"
