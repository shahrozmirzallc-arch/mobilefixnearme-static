Water Damaged Phone? Here's What to Do IMMEDIATELY

Last updated: January 2024 | Reading time: 6 minutes

Dropped your phone in water? Every second counts! Water damage is one of the most serious phone issues and requires immediate action. Whether it's a toilet drop, pool accident, or rain exposure, here's your complete survival guide.

🚨 CRITICAL: DO THESE IMMEDIATELY (First 60 Seconds)!

  1. Turn off the phone RIGHT NOW - Don't even check if it works!
  2. Remove case and SIM card tray - Water trapped inside makes damage worse
  3. Wipe dry with cloth - Get all visible water off
  4. Don't shake or blow into ports - This pushes water deeper inside
  5. Call Mobile Fix Near Me: 343-703-6000 - We respond in 30 minutes!

❌ NEVER Do These Things (They Make It Worse!):

  • ❌ Don't turn it on to "test" it - This causes short circuits!
  • ❌ Don't charge it - Electrical current + water = permanent damage
  • ❌ Don't use a hair dryer - Heat damages internal components
  • ❌ Don't put it in rice - This is a MYTH! Rice doesn't work and dust gets inside
  • ❌ Don't shake or tap it - Water spreads to undamaged areas
  • ❌ Don't wait "to see if it dries" - Corrosion starts within hours!

💧 Types of Water Damage:

1. Fresh Water (Tap, Rain, Puddles):

Danger Level: Medium - Acts quickly but less corrosive

2. Salt Water (Ocean, Pool with chlorine):

Danger Level: EXTREME - Highly corrosive, damages circuitry fast

3. Other Liquids (Soda, Coffee, Juice):

Danger Level: High - Sugar and acids cause sticky residue and corrosion

💡 Professional Insight

The "Rice Trick" is a DANGEROUS MYTH!

Putting your phone in rice does NOT fix water damage. Here's why:

  • Rice doesn't absorb water from inside the phone effectively
  • Dust and rice particles get into ports and cause more damage
  • You waste critical time while corrosion sets in
  • Modern phones are sealed - external drying doesn't help internal water

The ONLY solution is professional cleaning and repair within 24-48 hours!
