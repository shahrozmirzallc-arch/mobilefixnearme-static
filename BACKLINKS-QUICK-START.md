## 🔥 PRO TIPS

1. **Use same business info everywhere** (NAP consistency)
   - Name: Mobile Fix Near Me
   - Address: Service area (not physical)
   - Phone: Same number everywhere

2. **High-quality photos**
   - Logo (square, 500x500px)
   - Cover photo (1200x628px)
   - Service photos

3. **Complete all profile fields**
   - More info = Better SEO
   - Add hours, services, payment methods

4. **Ask for reviews** (especially Google!)
   - Reviews = Trust = Rankings

5. **Be patient**
   - Backlinks take 2-4 weeks to show in Google
   - Check progress in Google Search Console
