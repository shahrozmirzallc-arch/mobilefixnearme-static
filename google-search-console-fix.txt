🔍 WHY THIS FIXES THE ERRORS:
-----------------------------

Google doesn't accept aggregateRating inside LocalBusiness unless:
1. It's from verified third-party reviews (Google My Business)
2. It's properly structured as separate Review schema

By removing it:
✅ No more "invalid object type" error
✅ No more "multiple aggregate ratings" error
✅ Your actual Google My Business reviews will show instead
✅ All other rich results remain active (FAQ, Service, Breadcrumb)
